Ao Li (李奡)

Hi! I am a first-year master at Tsinghua University, majoring in Artificial Intelligence. Now I am doing research advised by Prof. Yansong Tang and Prof. Jiwen Lu at THU-IVG Lab. I received my B.S. degree in Artificial Intelligence from Beijing Normal University in 2024. Before that, I worked as an intern in BNU-IVC Lab advised by Prof. Yongzhen Huang and Prof. Saihui Hou, doing research in the field of gait recognition.

My research interests lie in the fields of Human-Robot Interaction. My current research focuses on human mesh recovery and human-object interaction.
